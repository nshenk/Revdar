Revdar - Revolution Radar


Revdar is a live global map of protests.

It works by using the GDELT database to index all web articles related to protests that are from the past 12 hours. From this it selects the top 75 locations with the highest article count, and places them on the map, with a heat size directly proportional to its article count.

For each location, a relevant picture is selected and a gallery of related articles generated.

While fairly accurate, there are always locations included where protests are not occurring. This is because it's difficult to distinguish whether an article is talking about a past or present protest, and whether the locations mentioned in the article are where the protest is actually taking place.

As we continue development, the accuracy will continue to rise; however, it's important to note that Revdar is to be used as a first resource before doing more research on specific protests.
